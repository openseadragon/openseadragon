Custom OpenSeadragon demo with arbitrary level scales and per-level tile sizes, plus three presets to stress-test the pyramid.
Convention: level 0 is the lowest-res, level N-1 is the highest-res.
For OpenSeadragon, typically the highest level has scale ≈ 1.0 and lower levels < 1.0.
Presets:
A = deep pyramid with small tiles; B = non-power-of-two scales & very large tiles; C = alternating tiny/huge tile sizes and uneven scales.
